Security & Verification

How to verify your download is authentic and safe

SHA256 Checksum Verification

Each download includes a unique SHA256 checksum. Verify your file matches to ensure integrity and authenticity.

Open PowerShell and run:

Get-FileHash -Algorithm SHA256 -Path "C:\path\to\your\file.exe"

Compare the output hash with the checksum shown on this page.

Open Terminal and run:

shasum -a 256 /path/to/your/file.dmg

Compare the output hash with the checksum shown on this page.

Open Terminal and run:

sha256sum /path/to/your/file.AppImage

Compare the output hash with the checksum shown on this page.

Digital Signatures

All executables are digitally signed to verify authenticity and prevent tampering during transmission.

  • Windows executables are signed with our authenticode certificate
  • macOS applications are notarized by Apple
  • All builds are verified against virus databases
