Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you use VarStream

Launched: November 22, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

VarStream is designed as a lightweight, mostly offline scripting tool. We collect only the minimum information needed to operate our website, deliver downloads, and improve the stability of the VarStream engine. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. When You Use the VarStream Application

The VarStream executable is designed to run locally on your machine and process .vat script files offline. By default, the VarStream app:

  • Does not upload your scripts, variables, or command-line input to our servers
  • Does not require you to create an account or log in
  • Does not access your personal documents or other folders unless you explicitly run VarStream there

In some cases, you may choose to send us logs or crash reports manually (for example, by attaching them to an email). In that case, those logs may contain:

  • Technical error messages and stack traces
  • Information about your operating system and VarStream version
  • File paths or parts of your script included in the error trace

1.2. When You Visit the VarStream Website

When you visit our website (including pages such as download.html, install.html, and this Privacy Policy), we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Analytics: Pages viewed, time on page, clicks, and general navigation patterns
  • Log Data: IP address (short-term logs), browser type, operating system, and referrer URL
  • Technical Details: Screen resolution, language preference, and basic device information

1.3. Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us or fill out forms, you may choose to provide:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address when you request support or send feedback
  • Support Details: Description of issues, screenshots, or sample scripts you share with us
  • Feedback: Suggestions, feature requests, or general comments about VarStream

1.4. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect or store:

  • Passwords, banking details, or payment card information
  • Content of your VarStream scripts, except if you deliberately send them to us for support
  • Location data from GPS, camera, or microphone feeds
  • Contacts, SMS, or personal communications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1. Operating and Improving VarStream

  • To provide downloads and installation instructions
  • To understand which OS platforms and versions are most used
  • To troubleshoot crashes, bugs, and performance issues
  • To plan new features and improvements for the VarStream engine

2.2. Support and Communication

  • To respond to support tickets and technical questions
  • To notify you about important changes, security fixes, or critical updates
  • To send optional product news or tips if you have opted in

2.3. Security and Abuse Prevention

  • To protect our website and infrastructure against abuse or attacks
  • To detect suspicious or automated traffic patterns
  • To enforce our Terms & Conditions and comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only in the following situations:

3.1. Service Providers

We may use third-party providers for analytics, hosting, or support tools. These providers may process:

  • Aggregated usage statistics for our website
  • Support tickets and emails you submit
  • Basic technical information necessary to deliver the service

All such providers are required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

3.2. Legal and Safety Requirements

We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of VarStream, our users, or the public
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues

3.3. Business Transfers

If VarStream or its assets are involved in a merger, acquisition, or transfer, limited user-related information may be part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy or an equivalent one.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold.

4.1. Technical Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for website traffic
  • Access controls for internal tools and logs
  • Regular updates and security patches for server software

4.2. Organizational Measures

  • Limiting access to user information to team members who need it
  • Internal guidelines on handling support data and logs
  • Deletion of logs and support messages once they are no longer needed

No system can be 100% secure, but we work to minimize risk and respond quickly to potential issues.

5. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purposes described above.

5.1. Typical Retention Periods

  • Web server logs: Typically retained for a short period for security and debugging, then deleted or anonymized
  • Analytics data: Kept in aggregated form for up to 24 months
  • Support emails: Retained for up to 3 years for reference and service quality
  • Crash logs sent by you: Retained only as long as needed to diagnose and fix the issue

5.2. Deletion on Request

You may request that we delete your personal information where applicable. We will remove or anonymize data unless we are required to keep it by law or for legitimate business reasons (for example, security logs).

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region (for example, the EU/EEA, UK, or California), you may have certain rights over your personal data, including:

6.1. Access and Correction

  • Right to know what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to receive a copy of your personal data
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

6.2. Control and Deletion

  • Right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Right to object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Right to opt out of marketing or non-essential communications
  • Right to data portability in some cases

6.3. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@example.com

We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The VarStream website may use cookies or similar technologies to function correctly and understand how visitors use the site.

7.1. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (such as keeping your selected language)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand page visits and general visitor flows
  • Preference Cookies: Remember basic settings and preferences

7.2. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies using your browser settings. If you disable some types of cookies, parts of the site may not function as intended.
